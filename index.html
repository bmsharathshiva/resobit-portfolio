How a ride works — end to end
From user tap to auto-lock, our firmware and backend handle every step.
📱
User Opens App
Map shows nearby bikes via real-time GPS. BLE scan begins on approach.
🔓
Digital Unlock
App sends BLE command to smart lock. QR fallback if BLE fails. Lock status confirmed via MQTT.
🚴
Active Ride
GPS pings at 1Hz. Live position streamed to app. Fare meter runs. Geofence zones monitored.
📍
Park & Lock
Auto-lock triggers in approved zone. Parking outside zone? App blocks ride completion.
💳
Auto Billing
Trip summary generated. Wallet deducted. Receipt sent. Fleet map updated in real-time.
